About American Corner Mahajanga

A space dedicated to cultural exchange, information, and educational support for the Mahajanga community. We connect Madagascar and the United States through accessible and inclusive programs.

Our offerings include: information about studying in the United States, library resources, English workshops, cultural events, and a modern attendance tracking system to facilitate activity management.

Contact Us View Members
Our Mission

Promote mutual understanding and access to educational and cultural resources between Madagascar and the United States.

Our Vision

To be a recognized local hub for learning, innovation, and cultural exchange known for excellence and inclusion.

Our Values

Accessibility, respect, collaboration, and transparency in all our actions.

Our History

  • 2018 — Launch of the local initiative in partnership with the U.S. Embassy and the University of Mahajanga.
  • 2019 — Deployment of the first English workshops and opening of the public library.
  • 2021 — Adoption of digital tools for member and event management.
  • 2024 — Launch of the QR code check-in system and expansion of community programs.
